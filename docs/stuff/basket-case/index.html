Basket Case

Looking for a stylish bag to fit your bicycle basket?  Look no further. There's a lot of bags out there but none (that I've discovered anyway) just right for throwing in a bicycle basket and heading out to your various business. 

I've been looking for this bag for a while.  Rather than spend my precious internet time searching for it, I decided to make one for myself.  And now that I have, I'm so pleased with the outcome that I'd like to offer it to you.  The Basket Case is designed to fit all the stuff you would stuff into a basket - specifically the wald 137 - in a stylish waterproof package.

It's made with Martexin Wax heavy cotton duck and ykk waterproof zippers.  Inner stiffener/shaper cut from super thick #4 duck for extra toughness.  Thick cotton straps for extra grippy shoulder hold.  Pockets on each side for easy access.  Contoured top for style.

Dimensions

Width: 12.5"

Depth: 9.5"

Height: 10.5" 

Materials

  • Martexin Original Wax 18 oz. heavy cotton duck 
  • Cotton Webbing
  • YKK Aquaguard Zippers
